A simple eCommerce platform for physical and digital products that allows sellers to build their shop quickly. Features includes unlimited variants, built in messaging system, risk scores, website embed and more.

Passed Box

A client-side encrypted digital deposit box that sends keep-alive messages and automatically sends to your designated beneficiary.

Technologies: Google Cloud, VueJS/TypeScript, Go, Stripe, SendGrid, TailwindCSS

Go to Passed Box

Document Management System

A document management system for a car dealership systems provider with scanning solution, flexible search engine and metadata, with DMS third party integration.
