No hidden fees. No surprises.

Our Simple Pricing Model

Service Fee @ $200/hr

We charge a flat rate of $200/hr for a minimum of $32,000 for 4 weeks of full software development service.

A 25% down payment is due prior to the start of the project. After that, the remaining balance will be due upon each project milestone completion. This process ensures that both client and the development team are on the same page throughout the project duration.

Server And Infrastructure Costs

Although your server/infrastructure configuration is a service we offer, the cost depends on the client’s chosen service provider. Payment for these services is not included on our service fee.

Third Party Service Integration Costs

Likewise, third party service integration is included in our offerings. However, the cost for any third party services such as Stripe/Stripe Connect, SendGrid, etc is the client’s responsibility.
